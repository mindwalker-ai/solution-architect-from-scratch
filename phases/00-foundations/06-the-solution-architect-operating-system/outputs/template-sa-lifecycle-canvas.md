# SA Lifecycle Canvas — Template

A one-page operating system for a deal. Copy this file into a new deal folder, rename it
`<customer>-sa-lifecycle-canvas.md`, and fill it in **top to bottom**. The discipline is
in the **Exit criteria** column: you do not advance to the next stage until its exit
criteria are met. If a stage's exit criteria can't be met, that is a signal — most often
to loop back to Discovery, or to **no-bid** at the Qualify gate.

> How to use: fill the **Exit criteria** column *first* (what would have to be true to move
> on), then work the deal until each row is satisfied. Update the **Status** as you go
> (`⬚ not started · ◐ in progress · ✅ met · ⛔ blocked`).

## The canvas

| # | Stage | Objective / activity | Artifact produced | Exit criteria (gate to advance) | Owner | Status |
|---|-------|----------------------|-------------------|---------------------------------|-------|--------|
| 1 | **Lead** | Confirm the opportunity is real and ours to pursue | Qualified-lead note | Named sponsor + plausible fit + AE assigned | AE + SA | ⬚ |
| 2 | **Discovery** | Understand the problem, stakeholders, current vs future state, constraints | Discovery notes | The *problem*, the *metric* it moves, and the hard constraints are written down | SA | ⬚ |
| 3 | **Qualify (MEDDICC)** | Decide if the deal is winnable *and* worth winning | MEDDICC scorecard + **bid / no-bid** | Economic buyer identified, champion confirmed, no disqualifier → **BID** | SA + AE | ⬚ |
| 4 | **Solution Design (HLD→LLD)** | Design the solution shape that meets the decision criteria | HLD (then LLD) | HLD maps 1:1 to the decision criteria; no requirement unaddressed | SA | ⬚ |
| 5 | **Sizing & BOM** | Size the workload and turn it into buyable line items | Sizing sheet + BOM | Sizing shows assumptions + a range; BOM priced with a sanity check | SA | ⬚ |
| 6 | **PoC** | Prove it works on the customer's data/use case | PoC plan + success criteria + results | Pre-agreed success criteria measurably met (or a clear "close enough") | SA + customer | ⬚ |
| 7 | **Proposal & Pricing** | Package why-us / why-now / what-return for the buyer | Proposal + exec summary + TCO/ROI | Buyer confirms the proposal maps to their decision criteria and budget | SA + AE | ⬚ |
| 8 | **Demo** | Make the value tangible for the decision-makers | Demo script + environment | The decision-maker (not just the champion) has seen and reacted | SA | ⬚ |
| 9 | **Negotiate** | Land the smallest winnable scope that de-risks delivery | Revised BOM / SOW + negotiation plan | Scope, price, phasing, and SLAs agreed; verbal commit | AE + SA | ⬚ |
| 10 | **Handoff** | Transfer to delivery with zero surprises | LLD + runbook + implementation plan + handoff notes | Delivery accepts the design as buildable as-sold | SA → Delivery | ⬚ |

## Artifact-chain checklist

Tick each link as it's produced. Each depends on the one above it — an empty upstream box
means the next artifact is a guess.
